About Korina

Hello there mi beautiful gente! My name is Korina Esther Moreno. I grew up in City Heights, San Diego and moved out to San Francisco when I was eighteen. Coming out here, experiencing a different air, and taking Latinx classes taught me a lot about who I am as a Latina woman. I now understand the importance of sharing our stories, as well as documenting our gentes stories. It is important to see where we all stand living del otro lado. I created this brand to help women of color get empowered and inspired to do more with their lives. It is about time that latinx women have a voice and are heard.
